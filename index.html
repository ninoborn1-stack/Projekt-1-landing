Features

Built for how you actually work.

No onboarding docs needed. Klar adapts to your workflow — not the other way around.

Instant task capture

Add tasks in seconds with keyboard shortcuts or voice. Never lose an idea again — Klar keeps everything in one place.

AI-powered prioritization

Let Klar surface what matters most today. Our AI analyzes deadlines, blockers, and team capacity automatically.

Real-time collaboration

Assign, comment, and update together — live. No more stale spreadsheets or tangled email threads.

Visual timelines

See your project from start to finish. Drag-and-drop milestones. Spot bottlenecks before they happen.

Focus mode

Silence the noise. Focus mode hides everything except what you committed to doing right now.

100+ integrations

Connects with Slack, GitHub, Notion, Figma and more. Klar fits into your stack — not the other way around.
